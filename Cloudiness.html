Cloudiness

The cloudiness versus latitude graph is difficult to decipher. The are many linear points on the graph meaning that cloudiness remains stagnant as latitude increases. This applies to both high and low levels of cloudiness. This may be due to precipitation differences in warmer climates as it tends to rain more in tropical climates, more clouds, than desert climates, less clouds.
